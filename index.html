About Us

Nearly Two Decades of Honest, Quality Building

Van Wiggen Builders was founded in 2007 by Rob Van Wiggen, a Licensed Building Practitioner with a straightforward philosophy: build it right, communicate honestly, and stand behind the work.

Since then we've grown into a trusted name across Christchurch and Canterbury, delivering quality new builds, renovations, and extensions for families who want a builder they can rely on from first consultation to final handover.

Every project - big or small - gets the same attention to detail, transparent fixed-price quoting, and hands-on involvement from Rob himself.
